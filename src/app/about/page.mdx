# About R2M

R2M is an independent research and product lab. We build long-horizon AI
systems — systems that reason, remember, reflect, and improve over time.

## What we're exploring

- **Long-horizon agents** — agents that stay coherent across hours, days, and
  weeks of work, pursuing goals that don't fit in one context window.
- **Memory systems** — persistent memory that accumulates experience and turns
  an agent's history into its advantage.
- **Autonomous research** — systems that form hypotheses, run experiments,
  reflect on the results, and refine their own understanding.
- **Reliable behavioral control** — long-running autonomy demands
  predictability: steerable, auditable, and safe to trust with real work.
